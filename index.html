About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer based in Portland, OR. After 20+ years working in kitchens including several years as an Executive Chef, leading teams, building systems, and running high pressure environments, I made a deliberate pivot into tech.

I completed General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive and found that the skills that made me a strong chef translate well to software development: breaking down complex problems, building efficient systems, and staying calm under pressure. I'm looking forward to picking up new tools and adapting to new technologies, and I am genuinely excited to keep expanding my stack.

Outside of coding, you'll find me on a trail, experimenting in the kitchen (full-snack engineer), or playing and enjoying live music.
